About The Artist

My name is Victoria. I am a homeschooling mom, U.S. Air Force Veteran, and M.S. Data Science Student. I love what I do. I started crocheting in 2014 as a way to cope with the struggle of returning from service after being medically retired. I learned by following along with YouTube videos until I got the hang of it. Who knew I would fall in love with the art. It has since become more than a hobby, and now provides for my family.

Thank you for checking out my business, and thank you for your support.
